Payment Terms & Conditions

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Payment Methods

We accept the following payment methods:

  • Cash on Delivery (COD)
  • UPI Payments (PhonePe, Google Pay, Paytm)
  • Bank Transfer / NEFT / RTGS
  • Debit/Credit Cards

2. Payment Terms

  • Payment is due at the time of order placement or delivery, depending on the payment method chosen
  • For bulk orders, advance payment of 50% may be required
  • All prices are in Indian Rupees (INR) and include applicable taxes

3. Order Confirmation

Orders will be confirmed once payment is received and verified. You will receive a confirmation via phone or WhatsApp.

4. Delivery Charges

  • Free delivery for orders above ₹2,000 within 20km radius
  • Delivery charges apply for smaller orders or distant locations
  • Delivery timeframes: 1-3 business days for local areas

5. Return & Refund Policy

  • Products can be returned within 7 days if unused and in original packaging
  • Damaged or defective products will be replaced free of charge
  • Refunds will be processed within 7-10 business days
  • Agricultural chemicals and opened packages are non-returnable

6. Pricing

All product prices are subject to change without notice. Prices displayed on our website or communicated by our team are final at the time of order confirmation.

7. Disputes

8. Cancellation Policy

  • Orders can be cancelled before dispatch without any charges
  • Once dispatched, cancellation may incur delivery charges
  • Contact us immediately if you wish to cancel your order
